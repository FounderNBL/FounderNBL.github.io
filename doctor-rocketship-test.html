The Doctor/Rocketship Test

A Category-Audit Method for Bad Analogies, Category Switches, and Arguments That Sound Strong Until Inspected

Did you ever think you had it all figured out?

Then you took the test and still failed?

Maybe the problem was not that every answer was wrong.

Maybe the problem was that you were taking the wrong test and did not know it.

Some arguments feel strong because they ask the wrong question in the right voice. They make you defend an answer before you realize the category changed.

The Doctor/Rocketship Test helps you slow that down.

Because sometimes the issue is not that you answered wrong.

Sometimes the issue is that nobody told you what room the question belonged in.
