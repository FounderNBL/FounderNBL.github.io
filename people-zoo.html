Some cages do not have bars.

Some cages have comfort, rules, smiles, routines, and a reason why you were chosen.

The People Zoo is a New Beansland story about curated environments, hidden control, and what happens when a place starts treating people like exhibits before they realize they were placed there.

What happens when the key holder has no lock to use?

This story asks what freedom means when the cage was designed to look like protection.
